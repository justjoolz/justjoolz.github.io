This is a selective account of what I actually invented and built—not a résumé with fashionable nouns added afterwards.

Current system · 01

THEØRB.IT Orbital Auction

THEØRB.IT explores an auction as a living system rather than a one-off contest. Participants, value and time interact through rules designed to make support visible and let a shared structure accumulate meaning.

My interest is in the mechanism beneath the interface: how a small set of constraints can create movement, feedback and unexpected forms of coordination without pretending the system is smarter than the people inside it.

The work spans protocol design, software, simulation and the language needed to make the mechanism legible. It is technical, but the underlying question is compositional: what must remain fixed, what should be allowed to move, and where does the space belong?
