02 Who is j00lz?

One inquiry.
Several decades.
No sensible genre.

I’m Julian Rutherford: Joolz to nearly everyone. I make music, design systems, take photographs and practise Vajrayana Buddhism.

The names and machines have changed—bass guitar, grooveboxes, record bags, Ableton, MPCs, software protocols—but the method has stayed oddly consistent: listen first, follow what has energy, leave enough room for something unexpected to enter.

This is the place where the tracks, mixes, stories and strange connections can finally live together.
